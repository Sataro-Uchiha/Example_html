About Itachi

Itachi says the remark for the first time in Chapter 391, during a talk with his younger brother Sasuke.He uses this remark to persuade Sasuke to abandon his fear of the unknown and confront his future with courage and resolve. It serves as a reminder not to be afraid of what we do not yet know or comprehend,but rather to approach new experiences with an open mind and a willingness to learn.
